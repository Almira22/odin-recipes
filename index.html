Polo

The Uyghur Polo is a traditional Uyghur Dish that is eaten by the Uyghur people all year round. Uyghurs, Uzbeks and some other Turkichic groups eat polo and it is traditionally eaten with bare hands.


Click Here for the Full Recipe on How to Make Polo

Here is List of Everything You Need to Make a Delicious Uyghur Polo:

  • carrots
  • meat of yours Choice (lamb or beef is recommended)
  • cooking oil
  • rice
  • garlic
  • onions

These Are the Steps to Making a Yummy Uyghur Polo!

  1. heat the pan with some cooking oil
  2. stir fry your meat till it's golden brown
  3. add chopped onions
  4. add chopped carrots
  5. add your seasoning
  6. add water and wait for it to boil
  7. add rice
  8. steam cook the dish
